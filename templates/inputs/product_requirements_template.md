# PRODUCT_REQUIREMENTS (产品需求模板)

## Meta (元信息)
- Baseline version: `draft|vX.Y`
- Author role: `author`
- Reviewer role: `reviewer`

## Product Goals (产品目标)
- `PR-*`: desired product outcome. (产品结果)
- Planned acceptance validation: `TC-A-*` family or method. (计划验收验证方式)

## User Scenarios (用户场景)
- Primary scenario and actor. (主要场景与角色)
- Trigger and expected outcome. (触发条件与预期结果)

## Scope In (范围内)
- In-scope deliverables. (范围内交付物)

## Scope Out (范围外)
- Explicit exclusions. (明确排除项)

## Constraints (约束)
- Delivery, platform, compliance, or schedule constraints. (交付、平台、合规或进度约束)

## Acceptance Outcomes (验收结果)
- `PR-*` -> observable product outcome. (可观察产品结果)
- Planned acceptance evidence or check. (计划证据或检查方式)

## Notes (备注)
- Open assumptions or product clarifications. (假设或澄清)
