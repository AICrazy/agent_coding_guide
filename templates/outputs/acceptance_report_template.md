# ACCEPTANCE_REPORT (验收报告模板)

## Scope Review (范围复核)
- In-scope outcomes completed. (范围内结果完成情况)
- Out-of-scope preserved. (范围外保持不变)

## Acceptance Validation Evidence (验收验证证据)
- `TC-A-*`: objective, method, result, evidence. (目标、方法、结果、证据)
- Related verification report refs. (关联验证报告)

## Product Outcome Coverage (产品结果覆盖)
- `PR-*`: outcome, method, result, evidence. (结果、方法、结论、证据)

## Gate Status (门禁状态)
- `G1` to `G5` summary. (阶段门禁总结)

## Risks and Defects (风险与缺陷)
- Remaining risks. (剩余风险)
- Blocker defects: `DEF-*` refs or `none`. (阻塞缺陷)
- Non-blocker defect summary or `none`. (非阻塞缺陷概述)

## Decision (结论)
- `accept|reject`. (通过或拒绝)
