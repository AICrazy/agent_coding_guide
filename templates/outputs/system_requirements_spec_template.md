# SYSTEM_REQUIREMENTS_SPEC (系统需求规格模板)

## Meta (元信息)
- Baseline version. (基线版本)
- Source product requirements doc. (来源产品需求文档)

## Functional Requirements (功能需求)
- `SYS-*`: system behavior. (系统行为)
- Source `PR-*`. (来源需求)
- Planned verification level: `unit|integration|system`. (计划验证层级)
- Acceptance validation is tracked separately at product level when needed. (验收验证在产品层单独跟踪)

## External Interfaces (外部接口)
- Interface, dependency, or runtime boundary. (接口、依赖或运行边界)
- Related `SYS-*`. (关联系统需求)

## Non-Functional Requirements (非功能需求)
- Performance, security, usability, compatibility, maintainability. (性能、安全、可用性、兼容性、可维护性)
- Related source `PR-*`. (来源需求)

## Risks and Assumptions (风险与假设)
- Delivery risk or unresolved assumption. (交付风险或未决假设)
