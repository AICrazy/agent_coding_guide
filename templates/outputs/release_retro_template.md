# RELEASE_RETRO (交付收尾模板)

## Scope (范围)
- Released or closed baseline version. (交付或关闭的基线版本)
- Covered scope summary. (覆盖范围概述)

## Gate Close-Out (门禁收尾)
- `G1` to `G5` final status summary. (门禁最终状态)
- Final acceptance decision ref. (最终验收结论引用)

## Changes and Rework (变更与返工)
- Related `CR-*` refs or `none`. (关联变更请求)
- Major rework loops and outcomes, or `none`. (主要返工闭环)

## Defects and Risks (缺陷与风险)
- Remaining non-blocker defects or `none`. (剩余非阻塞缺陷)
- Remaining accepted risks or `none`. (剩余已接受风险)

## Learnings (经验)
- Reusable lessons. (可复用经验)
- Knowledge note refs or `none`. (知识笔记引用)

## Follow-Up (后续事项)
- Deferred items or `none`. (延期事项)
- Owner and next trigger if applicable. (责任人与触发条件)
