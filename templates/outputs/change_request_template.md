# CHANGE_REQUEST (变更请求模板)

## Meta (元信息)
- `id`: `CR-*`
- requester. (提出人)
- date. (日期)
- status: `draft|approved|rejected|implemented|closed`

## Change Summary (变更概述)
- What changed. (变更内容)
- Why now. (变更原因)
- Urgency/priority. (紧急度/优先级)

## Impact (影响分析)
- Affected `PR-*|SYS-*|SWR-*`. (影响需求)
- Affected `SD-*|DD-*`. (影响设计)
- Affected artifacts. (影响工件)
- Impacted verification levels: `unit|integration|system`. (影响验证层级)
- Acceptance revalidation required: `yes|no`. (是否需要重新做验收验证)

## Baseline Decision (基线决策)
- Version change. (版本变化)
- Re-baseline required: `yes|no`. (是否需要重新基线)

## Closure (关闭)
- Approval result. (批准结果)
- Follow-up actions. (后续动作)
- Verification / validation evidence refs. (验证或验收证据引用)
