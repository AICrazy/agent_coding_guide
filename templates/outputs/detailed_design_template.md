# DETAILED_DESIGN (详细设计模板)

## Goal (目标)
- Implementation-ready design scope. (面向实现的设计范围)

## Modules (模块)
- `DD-*`: module or flow item. (模块或流程项)
- Responsibility. (职责)
- Mapped `SWR-*`. (对应软件需求)

## Data Structures and State (数据结构与状态)
- Key state/data shape. (关键状态或数据结构)
- Ownership and lifecycle. (归属与生命周期)

## Main Flows (主流程)
- Normal flow. (正常流程)
- Failure or fallback flow. (失败或降级流程)

## Testability Hooks (可测性设计)
- Observability, seams, fixtures, or deterministic controls. (可观测性、测试接缝、夹具或确定性控制)

## Risks and Follow-Up (风险与后续)
- Risks, deferred decisions, or future cleanup. (风险、暂缓决策或后续清理)
